Every data source delivers itself to a central reporting hub on its own schedule, in the cloud. The dashboard reads from the hub every time it's opened. No laptops involved, no exports, no one pressing refresh.

Our guardrails

Built in from day one — not added later.

Privacy by design

The dashboard shows totals and trends only. Names, emails, and phone numbers never leave GoHighLevel — drill-down into individual leads happens inside GHL, where your permissions apply.

View-only by design

Every data connection reads — it never writes. Nothing in this system can edit a campaign, change a budget, or touch a contact record.

Honest numbers

Every panel shows a “data as of” timestamp, and anything we can't attribute or don't have access to is labeled as such instead of being guessed or hidden.

Built to extend

New metrics, or an ad platform later if access ever opens up? The architecture is designed so additions are add-ons, not rebuilds.
